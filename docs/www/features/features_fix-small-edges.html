Fix small edges

The presence of small edges in your CAD model is a sort of dirtiness which often remain unnoticed until the part is passed in a subsequent workflow, such as Boolean operation or meshing. It is easy to check if your CAD part suffers from this anomaly by enabling visualization of vertices.

Use heal-small-edges Tcl command to merge the sequences of tiny edges into bigger primitives.
