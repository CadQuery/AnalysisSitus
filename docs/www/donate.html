Analysis Situs is a non-sponsored project being developed by a small research group in their spare time. This project has its own expenses the primary sources of whose are:

  • Web hosting.
  • Attending conferences as we think that is the best way to expose the development results (and, yeah, conferences are very expensive as they require flights/petrol, lodging, and fees; not to mention that all fun is held during the vacations of the involved persons).

You can support the project by donating to the corresponding PayPal account.
